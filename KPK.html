KHYBER PAKHTUN

"The land of the beautiful and the brave..."

Khyber Pakhtunkhwa or the hub of adventurers and explorers has a varied landscape ranging from rugged mountains, valleys, hills and dense agricultural farms. It has enough beauty and charm to keep you enthralled forever.The region is well known for its ancestral roots having a number of Hindu and Buddhist archaeological sites.

The Pakhtuns or the Pathans are considered to be one of the beautiful but bravest people.Strong and dignified is how they are categorized

Best Time to visit

May to September

The weather in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa is normally mild with cold freezing winters

None at present
