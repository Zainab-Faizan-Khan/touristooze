PUNJAB

"Culture , Colours , Music , Rivers ..Punjab has it all!"

Everybody knows Punjab! Its music cuisine and culture has no parallel.Its people are rather friendly jolly and colourful.

It has many landmarks of history and is home to some of the holiest places in Sikhism, Christanity and Islam. Its referred to as the Land of rivers having 5 rivers passing through it thus encompassing rich and fertile soil.

Best Time to visit

October to March

The weather in Punjab is normally hot and arid but improves during the winter and monsoon seasons
