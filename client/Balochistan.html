BALOCHISTAN

"A hidden gem , A treasure cove..."

Balochistan is the largest province both in terms of area enriched with various minerals and natural resources. It also has one of the largest and busiest port Gawadar which makes it an important point of trade.

The main ethnic people residing here are the Balochs that hold great resemblance with the iranian, which is why it is the LAND OF BALOCHS

Best Time to visit

Feburary to April
August to November

The weather in Balochistan is extremely cold in winter and scorching hot in summers
