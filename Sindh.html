SINDH

"Sindh is a gift of Indus as Egypt is the gift of Nile..."

Sindh the BABULISlAM has been a centre of cultural manifestations and a centre of handicrafts for many years.People are mostly generous and hospitable.

Sindh is the second largest province both in terms of population and area. The people are mostly Muslims but represtations of various religious communties reside in the province each with their special heritage

Best Time to visit

October to Feburary

The weather in Sindh is normally hot and humid but improves during the winter and monsoon seasons
